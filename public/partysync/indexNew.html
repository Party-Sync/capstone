The Who's & How's of PartySync

Adventurous, social souls that enjoy meeting new people and seeing new places! Perhaps you're new to town or just wanting to expand your circle of friends, PartySync is for YOU!


  1. Sign up!
    • After signing up/logging in: You now have full access!
      List a party or search for parties--Both are a breeze!
  2. Hosts:
    • Perhaps you had some last minute cancellations or you just want to meet new, like-minded friends. Simply fill-out the short Host form and you'll be all set to review potential guests.
  3. How to find Parties?
    • Easy! Click Search, type in the requested info, and Voila! Available parties are mapped - just contact the host for current availability, any specific requirements and party address.
  4. More questions?
    • Send an email to: hello@partysync.com
